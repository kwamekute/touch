Presidential Suite

Its location on the top floor means you can immerse yourself in the breathtaking views from its private balcony, while staying in massive Presidential Suite. Additionally, you can benefit from 24 hour butler service and complimentary access to the Executive Lounge with private check-in, dining and boardroom. This elegant, elevated suite features a kitchen with a separate entrance, living room with a spacious working area and a large balcony with lounge seating. Unwind in a comfortable king-size bed and refresh in the bathroom, which has a separate jaccuzi and shower. Choose from the large selection of international TV channels to be viewed on your 46-inch LCD TV, or connect with complimentary high-speed Internet access. Individually controlled air conditioning is provided. Our Presidential Suite can accommodate a maximum of three adults, or two adults and one child.
